# How-To and Examples

These files are optional craft guidance for players and DMs. They are seeds,
not requirements. The SRD has rules; methodologies have required workflows;
instructions have mechanical tool behavior.

## Files

- [`action-narration.md`](action-narration.md) — narrating rolls and pressure.
- [`constructive-secrets.md`](constructive-secrets.md) — hidden character
  information that enriches rather than sabotages.
- [`scene-hooks.md`](scene-hooks.md) — hooks, strong starts, and player-facing
  invitations.
- [`specificity.md`](specificity.md) — resisting generic drift with concrete
  world texture.

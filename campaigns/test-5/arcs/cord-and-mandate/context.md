# Cord and Mandate

The Half-Bar Run has fourteen weeks until the Conclave's ten-year audit. The Coremark Reach Survey expansion at Springreed is moving through its procedural steps — Tempered Accord environmental review filed, Conclave fragment-recovery agreement in negotiation. Auren Talindra has hand-delivered the audit-prep notice to Marn personally. Pell, the Recursive Weave handler, is at the head of the dock, watching from a polite distance.

It is the same week.

## What you know

- *Wickrun* is back from a Stillsink run. Cord at ~30% wear, one segment ticked.
- The audit cycle ends in 14 weeks.
- The Coremark Cut at Springreed will, eventually, break ground.
- The Stillsink Catalogue Mandate is moving in Sithari. If it passes, the Run loses private-commission carriage rights at next renewal.
- Yorek is at Tev's stall. His fourth fragment has been refused by the Conclave on procedural grounds.

## What's at stake

The carrier license. The crew. The Run as a thing the world has, or doesn't.

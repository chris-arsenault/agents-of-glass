# Wickrun

The Half-Bar Run's primary kite-hauler. Two crew bunks, four-berth common, a small mortuary bay sealed in lead-lined ringglass, a tuner array bonded into the hull frame.

## Description

A short-haul kite-hauler in the older Sable working class — squat, wide-bellied, faded blue paint with the *Wickrun* glyph hand-painted in white on the port side by Marn Vesh in 2117. Resonance-bonded ringglass array along the underside of the hull. Looks unremarkable in flight. Sounds wrong if you know what you're listening for.

## Provenance

Built 2089 in a Lowstep yard, original name *Tarnsail*. Sold three times. Bought by Marn Vesh in 2117 from a Conclave estate sale (the estate being one of the founding Conclave archivists; the ship had been used as a deep-archive transport for fifteen years). Marn renamed it *Wickrun* and refit the tuner array with help from **Eshaia** (former Half-Bar Run member, fae, no longer with the co-op).

## Current status

Active. Berth 4 at Lowstep dock. Cord set at ~30% wear (durable public clock: *Wickrun: Cord Wear* at 1/5). Hull integrity good. Tuner array calibrated to a profile nobody alive can fully replicate — Eshaia's hand. Joro has tried twice. The array still rings cold to her tuning.

## What it does

- **Mid-band-tuned kite-hauler.** Engine cores tuned to 2.8 kHz with a half-bandwidth of 80 Hz. Quieter than industry standard. Slower in hard wind.
- **Mortuary cargo bay** sealed for resonance-bonded ash and Echo River fragments. Lead-lined. The seal is rated for Conclave-grade fragment carriage.
- **Tuner array** in the hull frame, primary purpose: cancel ambient signal-band noise so cargo doesn't drift in transit. Side effect: anyone with Tuner training who runs the array cold can occasionally hear *Eshaia's* calibration as a thin overtone. PCs with the right specialty will notice.

## Hook

The array is unique. Coremark has, twice, asked about acquiring or licensing it. Marn has refused both times. The third time will be different. Also — the ship has *witnessed* every Half-Bar Run job since 2117. Voices have passed through that mortuary bay. Some of those voices are still, very faintly, in the lead.

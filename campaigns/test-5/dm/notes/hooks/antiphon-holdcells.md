# The Antiphon at the Holdcells

The Antiphon was seen at the Holdcells in 2133 with its hand on the Coremark wooden crate. The Recursive Weave plans to set up the Stillsink-node in the same lower channels six weeks from now. The Antiphon has not been seen at the Holdcells *since* 2133, but it has been seen at three nearby degraded surfacings in 2135.

## What's at stake if engaged

- A first encounter with the Antiphon on terms the party can shape. Possibly: figure out what the Antiphon wants. Possibly: find out what's in the crate.
- The Weave's node setup is dramatically complicated.

## What's at stake if ignored

- The Weave's setup crew encounters the Antiphon during the antenna-array bonding (faction-clock segment 4). One Weave specialist loses a sentence permanently. Pell calls Marn the next day.

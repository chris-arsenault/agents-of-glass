# The Mereth Shard transfer order

If the Stillsink Catalogue Mandate passes, the Mereth Shard's field copy must transfer to Sithari within 30 days. Auren Talindra will need to physically hand it off — likely to a Conclave courier flown in from Sithari. The Run is one of two carriers in the region capable of the carriage.

## What's at stake if engaged

- The Run is asked, by Auren personally, to be the carrier.
- Or the Run is approached by the Recursive Weave — *quietly*, *during* the carriage — to copy the shard before delivery.
- The Run could refuse, copy, deliver clean, deliver compromised, or never deliver.

## What's at stake if ignored

- The other regional carrier (a smaller, less reliable outfit) takes the job. The shard arrives in Sithari intact. The Sable Office's last live counter-leverage is buried in the Sithari deep archive.

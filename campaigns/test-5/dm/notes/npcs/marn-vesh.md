# Marn Vesh

Hab-Worlder, mid-forties. Founder and current head of the Half-Bar Run. Former Conclave field-archivist, fired in 2114 for an unsanctioned listen. The Run's eight-person crew think she "washed out." She did not. She let them think it.

## Tell

She carries a chipped enamel mug to every meeting and sets it down deliberately. Soft set: the conversation is going to keep going. Hard set: the meeting is over and she's already decided.

## Wants

- **Near:** clear the Conclave audit (14 weeks). Pay off the *Wickrun* refit.
- **Mid:** decide whether to take the Recursive Weave's Stillsink-node carriage offer. She has been not-deciding for six weeks.

## Fears

- That the Run becomes a cult of personality and dies with her bad lungs. She is already coughing on cold mornings; the crew has noticed.

## Bond / hook

- Was an apprentice to **Auren Talindra** at the Sable Office in 2110-2114. They have not spoken since 2114. Auren still files Run audits.
- Owes a personal debt to **Tev Korrish** (Lowstep dockmaster) whose brother went to drift on a Half-Bar Run job in 2129.
- Has a long phone-tag relationship with **Pell**, the Weave handler. Marn calls; Pell does not always call back. Marn finds this restful.

## Next move

This week, if no PCs touch her: she takes a slow walk around *Wickrun*'s berth at Lowstep, drinks one cup of tea at Tev's stall, and reschedules the Pell call for the third time. Next week she writes a letter to Auren that she will not send.

# Tev Korrish

Hab-Worlder, fifty-three. Lowstep's senior dockmaster. Runs the small tea stall at the foot of Berth 4 — *Wickrun*'s berth — which is technically a code violation that nobody has cited him for in fifteen years.

## Tell

Closes one eye when he's tallying. The other eye is a clear blue and unsettling to be tallied by.

## Wants

- **Near:** keep the dock running. Keep the small berths from being rolled up into Coremark's expansion plans (he has been hearing rumors).
- **Mid:** marry off his niece to someone she actually likes. (His niece is **Joren Pell**, the dockhand, who he does not know is feeding Coremark security and the Weave simultaneously.)

## Fears

- His brother, who went to drift in 2129 on a Half-Bar Run mortuary job, was not safe-recovered. Tev believes — has come to believe — that his brother is partly inside the *Wickrun* tuner array. He has never said this to Marn. He once asked Joro about the array's harmonic profile. Joro, kindly, gave a non-answer.

## Bond / hook

- Marn Vesh owes him a debt. He has never collected it. He keeps it in his pocket like the chipped enamel mug.
- His niece Joren Pell is a dockhand and, unbeknownst to Tev, a triple agent (Coremark, Weave, the dock).

## Next move

This week: hears, from a Coremark site clerk who ate at his stall, that the Reach Survey expansion may include a "berth consolidation" review. Says nothing. Closes the stall an hour early Friday. Walks the dock.

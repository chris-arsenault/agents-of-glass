# Vendrik Solvaen

Sitharian, mid-forties. Coremark Reach Survey site lead for the Sable Crescent. Reports directly to Coremark central in Sithari. Lives on-site at Reach Survey HQ; visits Sithari one weekend a month.

He is not a villain. He is a competent industrial manager who took the job because his daughter has a chronic resonance-damage condition that requires Sithari-grade hospital care, which Coremark's executive medical plan covers. He believes the Cut is the right call. He believes the Sable Crescent communities are being unreasonable. He believes both things sincerely.

## Tell

Wears a small ringglass pendant around his neck, never visible — the chain disappears under his collar. The pendant is a tuned shard that hums when his daughter is awake (paired-resonance keepsake; Lyse, age nine, calibrated it as a school project two years ago). PCs who get close enough to see his throat will see the chain. PCs who get into a quiet room with him will hear the pendant.

## Wants

- **Near:** sign the Conclave fragment-recovery agreement. Break ground at Springreed. (Faction clock segments 2-3.)
- **Mid:** advance to Coremark central in Sithari within two years.

## Fears

- His daughter's condition worsening. The job covers her care. He cannot afford to lose it.

## Bond / hook

- Reports up to **Mer Korvanis** (Coremark central, in Sithari). Mer is one of the two council members feeding Coremark intel on the Stillsink Catalogue Mandate.
- Has personally hired Pell-affiliated dockhand **Joren Pell** (no relation to the Weave handler) and is using him to pass selective intelligence to the Weave. He thinks of this as "managing the unmanageable."

## Stat-block

- **HP range:** 18 (he is not a fighter; he is an organizer with armed contractors).
- **Attack profile:** unarmed, untrained — but never alone. Two Reach Survey security personnel within hailing distance whenever he is on-site (each: HP 22, kite-cord net + signal-disrupter baton).
- **What makes him dangerous:** institutional reach, not personal force. He can have the Half-Bar Run's berth declared an "operational hazard zone" with three signatures and a phone call. He can have *Wickrun*'s license flagged for review. He can do these things faster than the party can react.
- **How he escapes if losing:** calls in security, then a phone call. He has never been in an actual physical confrontation. He will run.

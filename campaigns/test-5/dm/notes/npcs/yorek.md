# Yorek

Hab-Worlder, sixty-eight. Mononym in practice; "Yorek Sands" on legal papers. Retired kite-cord weaver from a small Sable settlement called Sands End that no longer formally exists (absorbed into Lowstep's administrative district six years ago). A recurring Half-Bar Run client.

## Tell

Wears one good wool coat that was his daughter's. The coat is two sizes too small for him. He never closes the buttons.

## Wants

- **Near:** the Run carries a fragment for him. He has paid for three so far. He wants a fourth. The Conclave has refused his fourth on procedural grounds (insufficient family-tree documentation; Sands End records were lost in 2129).
- **Mid:** to die having heard his daughter's last sentence. He has the first three sentences. He believes there's a fourth.

## Fears

- That the fourth fragment doesn't exist. That his daughter never said anything more. That what he wants is not real.

## Bond / hook

- A regular at Tev Korrish's tea stall. They sit together once a week and do not talk about Tev's brother or about Yorek's daughter.
- Has approached the Recursive Weave once. Pell turned him down — the Weave does not deal in fragments without strong family-tree evidence either, *anymore*, after the 2131 Fermata incident.

## Next move

This week: returns to the Sable Office in person (he does this every fourth week) and asks again for a procedural review. Auren Talindra signs his refusal personally. He thanks her. He leaves.

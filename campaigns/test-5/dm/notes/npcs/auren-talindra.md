# Auren Talindra

Sitharian, late fifties. Senior field-archivist at the Conclave's Sable Office, Lowstep branch. Files all Half-Bar Run audits personally — has done since 2110, when Marn Vesh was her apprentice.

## Tell

Wears a folded paper note in her left sleeve, never the right. The note is a reminder she rewrites every morning. Whatever it says, she touches it before she signs anything contested. PCs will see her touch her sleeve and not know why.

## Wants

- **Near:** to keep the Sable Office autonomous. To file a Run audit that closes clean. To finish the Mereth Korvanis catalogue project before she retires.
- **Mid:** to retire to a Sable Crescent fishing settlement with her partner. Within four years.

## Fears

- That the Stillsink Catalogue Mandate passes and the Sable Office secedes from the Conclave. She would have to choose. Her partner expects her to choose Sable. Her professional life expects Sithari.

## Bond / hook

- Marn Vesh's former mentor. They have not spoken since Marn's firing in 2114. Auren signed the dismissal. She still keeps Marn's apprenticeship-year fragment notebook in her office; nobody knows.
- Sits two chairs away from one of the two Conclave council members in Coremark's pocket — has noticed the woman's voting drift but has not yet acted on it.

## Next move

Files a routine Run audit-prep notice on schedule. Touches her sleeve. Files it.

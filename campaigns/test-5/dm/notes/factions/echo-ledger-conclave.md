# Echo Ledger Conclave — Campaign Layer

The world-bible entry covers what the Conclave is: institutional custodian of pre-Glassfall material surfacing in the Echo Rivers, system-wide academic body, jealous of access. This file is what the Conclave is *doing right now in this campaign*.

## One-sentence identity

The institutional witness who has been winning the access argument for ninety years and is starting to lose grip on the regional periphery.

## Goal — current

Pass the **Stillsink Catalogue Mandate** through the Conclave's own central council in the next four months. The Mandate would centralize all Sable Crescent fragment-cataloguing in Sithari and effectively shut down the regional Sable Office's autonomous releases — including private-commission carriage by independents like the Half-Bar Run.

The Mandate is being pushed from Sithari, opposed by the Sable Office, and quietly hated by the regional grievers' associations. The vote is mid-arc material.

## Tier

System-wide, upper-middle. Not as large as Coremark or the Tempered Accord. More legitimacy than money.

## Hold

- Custodianship of every catalogued fragment surfacing in the Echo Rivers system-wide. Storage, access protocols, release schedules.
- Carrier license issuance and audit. Half-Bar Run's license is theirs.
- Stillwater buoy distribution rights along the Sable boundary.
- A respected academic publication record going back ninety years.
- The deep Conclave archive vault under Sithari, where the *original* recovered fragments live. Nobody outside the Conclave has been in there in twenty years.

## Clock — Stillsink Catalogue Mandate (4 segments)

If nothing opposes the central council's push:

1. **Sable Office formally raises objection** — done; this is where the campaign starts. The objection is procedural and weak.
2. **Council circulates a draft.** Draft includes a "private-commission grandfather clause" that excludes existing carriers — the Half-Bar Run is *not* protected because their license was renewed two years ago and the clause cuts off at three years.
3. **Council vote scheduled.** Sable Office begins quietly preparing to leave the Conclave entirely. (This is real. They will.)
4. **Mandate passes.** Sable Office secedes or capitulates. Either way, the Half-Bar Run loses its private-commission carriage rights at the next renewal.

If the party engages: the clock can be slowed, redirected, or the Sable Office's secession can be steered toward something less catastrophic. If the party doesn't: the world keeps moving.

## Relationships

- **Coremark Reach Survey:** opposed in principle, cooperative in practice. Conclave gets first look at Coremark river-rock cores; Coremark gets a Conclave seal that lets them sell the processed feedstock at academic-grade prices.
- **Tempered Accord:** Accord-recognized; quietly resentful of Accord oversight attempts.
- **The Recursive Weave:** the Conclave officially does not acknowledge the Weave exists. They cancel members' licenses when caught dealing with it.
- **The Half-Bar Run:** known, watched, currently protected because no audit has caught the breaches. Audit is in 14 weeks.

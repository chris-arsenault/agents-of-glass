# Conclave Sable Office

The Echo Ledger Conclave's regional Sable Crescent branch, located in Lowstep's converted ring-era administrative building, three floors up the slope from the docks.

## What it feels like

Old paper, fresh ink, slow. Three floors of cataloguing rooms, a sign-in desk, a tertiary vault on the second floor, a third floor nobody discusses. The walls are ring-era stone, three meters thick, naturally signal-band-quiet — fragments stored here drift more slowly than fragments anywhere else on the Crescent.

## What's worth writing

- **Auren Talindra's office** on the second floor. Door open most hours. Her notebook collection lines the back wall, including the apprenticeship-year notebook of one **Marn Vesh**, kept on the third shelf from the top.
- The **tertiary vault** holds the field copies of regional fragment-shards, including the **Mereth Shard**. Two-key access; Auren and one other archivist.
- The **third floor** holds the Sable Office's *political* archive — internal correspondence with Sithari, dissenting opinions, planning material for the secession nobody is officially planning. Access by request, granted reluctantly.

## Who's usually there

- Auren Talindra (most days).
- Two junior field-archivists. One of them is a Coremark sympathizer; she does not know that everyone else has noticed.
- Yorek, every fourth Tuesday.
- A semi-regular flow of Half-Bar Run paperwork — sealing certificates, mortuary registrations, license-audit precursors.

## What does something

- The signal-band-quiet stone walls. A fragment played in this building does not drift. A meeting held in this building cannot be eavesdropped on with conventional equipment.
- The Mereth Shard is here. Its master is in Sithari.

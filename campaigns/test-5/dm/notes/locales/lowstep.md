# Lowstep

A working dock-and-warehouse town on the western edge of the Sable Crescent. Two thousand permanent residents. The Half-Bar Run's home berth.

## What it feels like

Wet stone underfoot most mornings. The smell of crystal sediment from the lower river-channels mixes with kite-cord hemp and frying saberreed. The dock pilings still carry the pre-Glassfall foundations — every fourth pylon hums faintly when wind comes off the water.

## What's worth writing

- **Berth 4** is *Wickrun*'s. Tev Korrish's tea stall sits at the foot of the berth, technically a code violation. The kettle has been on a continuous low burn for fifteen years.
- The **Conclave Sable Office** is up the slope from the docks, in a converted ring-era administrative building. Three floors. The third floor is restricted. Auren Talindra's office is on the second.
- The **Coremark Reach Survey "community engagement office"** is on the main commercial street, posted hours, never staffed when those hours match the dock schedule. Locals do not enter.

## Who's usually there

- Dockhands, including **Joren Pell** (Tev's niece, triple agent: Coremark security, Recursive Weave, the dock).
- Half-Bar Run crew. Marn lives in a small flat above the berth office.
- Yorek, every fourth Tuesday at the Sable Office. Tev's tea stall after.
- Coremark Reach Survey contractors transiting through; not residents but visible.

## What does something

- Every fourth pier-pylon hums in resonance with a distant Echo River channel that surfaces 11km inland. Locals have used the hum as a weather-tell for two generations.

# test-5 — Campaign Summary

**Question:** Who has the right to listen?
**Scarcity:** witnesses
**Setting:** Sable Crescent, surface of Kaleidos. 130 years after the Glassfall.

The party belongs to **The Half-Bar Run**, a small carrier co-op out of Lowstep that ferries Echo River fragments and other resonance-sensitive cargo. They are halfway through a Conclave ten-year audit cycle, working under economic pressure (cord wear on *Wickrun*, *Halfsole* in dock), watching three institutional pressures converge: a Coremark expansion at Springreed, a Conclave Catalogue Mandate that would centralize cataloguing in Sithari, and a quiet offer from the Recursive Weave to ferry a starter archive to a new underground node.

The opening arc — **Cord and Mandate** — is the six-week window in which all three pressures resolve, one way or another.

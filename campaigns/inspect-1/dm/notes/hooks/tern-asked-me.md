# "Tern asked me to look at something."

**Situation:** The station's lead maintenance Tuner has been logging anomalous readings from Mount 7 and 15 for three months. He's filed the reports through proper channels. Nobody has responded with anything that looks like engagement. He's now asking informally — through whoever the party knows, or through the party directly if they have any technical background — whether someone will just sit with him for an hour and look at the raw data.

He doesn't want help. He wants someone to look at the numbers and tell him he's wrong, or tell him he's right. He's been in his own head about this long enough that he can't trust his own read anymore.

**What's at stake if engaged:** The party sees the raw data. The raw data, to anyone with resonance training, is alarming. This opens threads: Ol'Rint, the Zone expansion, the pattern that matches three other stations. Tern becomes a cooperative information source.

**What's at stake if ignored:** Tern's personal log grows. He keeps showing up to work. In six weeks he's off the station, the anomaly goes with him, and whoever replaces him doesn't know what they're inheriting.

**Which NPCs/factions it touches:** Tern Molsk, Boundwatch, Ol'Rint, (indirectly) Senna Kovrath.

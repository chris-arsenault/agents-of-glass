# "The supply ship is three days late."

**Situation:** Saltmark hasn't arrived on schedule. The kite-hauler runs the supply route between Vestige Point and the Karet-adjacent Keel waystation on a reliable ten-day cycle. It's three days overdue. No distress signal. The last transmission placed it at the waystation, loading confirmed. Nothing since.

The Cooperative's logistics coordinator has filed it as "delayed, cause unknown" and is monitoring. They haven't contacted the waystation directly because doing so would require going through Vantara's communication relay, which creates a formal record, which creates questions about why a Cooperative supply hauler is delayed on a Council-adjacent contract.

**What's at stake if engaged:** The party goes to the waystation, or contacts it, or pressures the Cooperative to do so. They find Saltmark still docked. Dern Kask explains what's been happening at the outer arms — the Coremark-adjacent vessels, the twelve-to-twenty hour stops, something she saw two days ago that made her decide waiting was safer than leaving. What she saw was a Coremark vessel offloading what looked like resonance-contact equipment of the type used for tapping mid-bandwidth arrays. She doesn't know that's what it was. She knows it looked wrong.

**What's at stake if ignored:** Saltmark arrives two days later, captain close-mouthed, cargo correct. Dern Kask files nothing. The Cooperative's logistics coordinator files nothing. The Coremark staging at the waystation continues unobserved.

**Which NPCs/factions it touches:** Saltmark/Dern Kask, Karet Supply Cooperative, Coremark (presence), Karet-adjacent Waystation.

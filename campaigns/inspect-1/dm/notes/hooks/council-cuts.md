# "The Council is cutting staff."

**Situation:** Posted in the station's common area: a formal notice from the Displacement Council, confirmed by Senna Kovrath's upcoming visit. Vestige Point's monitoring crew will be reduced by 30%, effective six weeks from today. Three positions eliminated; the roles will be absorbed by existing Cooperative staff per a contract amendment pending Kovrath's approval.

The notice is legally valid, procedurally correct, and — given the timing and the anomaly — a significant problem. Tern Molsk is on the reduction list. So are two of the three current Council monitoring staff. The monitoring roles they vacate will be taken by Cooperative contractors who report to Duva.

**What's at stake if engaged:** The party has reason to engage with Senna Kovrath before she finishes her station review. If someone can get the raw anomaly data to Kovrath before she signs off on the reduction — and make the case that eliminating the person who flagged the anomaly and the monitoring staff capable of reading it is the wrong moment for this particular cut — Kovrath has the authority to defer the reduction for a review period. She won't want to. She will if the case is made clearly enough.

**What's at stake if ignored:** The reduction is approved. Tern's off the station in six weeks. Chel'sten's operation concludes before the reduction takes effect. The Cooperative's monitoring coverage begins, and the next cycle of anomaly data looks normal because the Cooperative controls what gets reported.

**Which NPCs/factions it touches:** Senna Kovrath, Tern Molsk, the Displacement Council, the Karet Supply Cooperative, the timing of everything.

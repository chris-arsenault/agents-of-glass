# Senna Kovrath

The Displacement Council's field representative for the Karet-3 cordon. Has been managing a containment crisis at Bloom Zone Pellani-7 for the past seven months, which is why she hasn't been at Vestige Point. She's about to return.

**Tell:** She speaks in the Sithari formal register — measured, complete sentences, no contractions — except when she's angry, at which point her sentences get shorter and shorter. Three words is very bad.

She's genuinely competent. She's also in a defensive crouch before she walks through the door, because she knows she should have been here and she knows she wasn't and she has a staff reduction order to execute and she's hoping the station is in better shape than she suspects.

She reviewed Tern Molsk's anomaly reports remotely, based on Duva's summary. She closed the file as "within normal parameters." She has not seen the raw data.

## Wants

To resolve whatever situation exists at Vestige Point before it becomes a formal incident report that reaches Dern Talish. She doesn't want to be the field representative on record for a cordon failure.

What she actually needs but won't say: someone to show her the raw data before she executes the staffing reduction.

## Fears

That the resources required to address the anomaly — if it's real — are resources she can't authorize without a formal escalation that would put her seven-month absence on record.

## Bond / Hook

Connected to: the Council (her employer, her constraint), Duva (she trusts his reports more than she should), Tern Molsk (she's aware he's on the reduction list, she's not aware he's the person who logged the anomaly), the staffing reduction order.

## Next Move

If the party never appears: she arrives at Vestige Point, tours the station with Duva, accepts his explanation for the anomaly reports, signs the staffing reduction, and leaves within forty-eight hours. The file stays closed. The monitoring gap opens in six weeks.

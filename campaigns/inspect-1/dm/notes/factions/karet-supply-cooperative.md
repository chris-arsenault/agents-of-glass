# Karet Supply Cooperative

*The contractors who actually run the station. Ostensibly independent. Three handshakes from Coremark.*

**Identity:** A mid-scale logistics and maintenance contractor that handles supply runs, crew rotation, facility maintenance, and increasingly station monitoring at Vestige Point. They have a four-month contract renewal pending. Their operations manager is Duva.

The Cooperative was incorporated six years ago from former Coremark logistics staff who went legitimate after the Bloom. Legitimate enough. The connection to Coremark is informal — shared investors, shared personnel, information flows in exchange for cash. Not a subsidiary. An arrangement.

## Goal (right now)

Secure the contract extension. The extension includes provisions that would give the Cooperative formal responsibility for monitoring roles currently held by Council staff — specifically the roles that will open when the staffing reduction hits. Once they hold those monitoring roles, they control what the monitoring data looks like.

Parallel goal (unsanctioned, not known to most Cooperative staff): complete three more data transfers from the Firstpost Buoy to Coremark. This is Chel'sten's operation, run with Duva's tacit non-knowledge.

## Hold

Day-to-day operational control of Vestige Point: supply logistics, maintenance scheduling, docking authority, crew accommodations. The secondary access shaft in the Deep Tap (not in the original blueprints) connecting to a duskgrain cache at the cordon boundary.

## Clock (5 segments)

1. Duva buries an unfavorable inspection report under four layers of bureaucracy (done — this has already happened)
2. Contract extension signed with expanded monitoring provisions
3. Chel'sten completes the final data transfer and disappears
4. Cooperative takes over vacated monitoring roles; cordon edge data goes dark from Council's perspective
5. Large-scale Coremark duskgrain extraction begins; Bloom Zone boundary behavior changes

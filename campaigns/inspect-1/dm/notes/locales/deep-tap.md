# The Deep Tap

A Shear-side access point at the bottom of Vestige Point's structural framework. The station's lowest level — accessed through a maintenance ladder that runs sixty meters below the inhabited decks. Cold, quiet, and outside the station's main resonance-regulation zone. Equipment hums differently here; the isolation from the array's influence makes the ambient Shear resonance more present.

**Official function:** Maintenance access to the Boundwatch array's deep mounts, specifically Mount 7 and Mount 15 (which are anchored in the station's lower structure rather than on the perimeter hull). Required for physical inspection of the mounts' crystal connections.

**The secondary shaft:** The Cooperative added a secondary access shaft that isn't in Vestige Point's original blueprints. It runs from the Deep Tap's maintenance floor to an access port on the station's exterior hull, approximately eighty meters toward the cordon perimeter. The shaft is rated for standard maintenance access. The access logs show twelve entries in the past two months. The Cooperative's official records show one.

**What's at the end of the shaft:** The external access port opens approximately 40 meters from the Firstpost Buoy. During specific resonance conditions, the port is close enough to the Firstpost Buoy's access panel to run a physical-contact resonance line — which is how Chel'sten conducts the data transfers without triggering the wireless monitoring protocols.

**At the port's exterior:** The duskgrain cache. A small deposit — a few hundred grams total — that has crystallized in the boundary region near this access point. The Threshold Pack passes near here regularly. The deposit has been growing, not diminishing.

**Atmosphere:** The Deep Tap is the one place on the station where the Bloom Zone is audible — a low, irregular harmonic that the station's upper decks filter out. Tuners find it uncomfortable. Non-Tuners often don't notice it. People who spend time here regularly develop mild headaches and stop mentioning them.

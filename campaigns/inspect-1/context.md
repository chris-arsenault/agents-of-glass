# Vestige Point

The party is stationed at or near Vestige Point — a Bloom Zone cordon station in the Shear, adjacent to Bloom Zone Karet-3. The station runs Boundwatch, the ringglass monitoring array that forms this section of Lira's Wall. It has been operational for eight years.

**The question the campaign is asking:** *What do you owe a thing you've kept alive past the point where someone should have said it out loud?*

**The scarcity:** trust — specifically, the trust that lets information move between people who need it.

---

Three hundred people live here. The Displacement Council holds formal authority; the Karet Supply Cooperative runs day-to-day operations. The field representative from Council hasn't been on station in seven months. The station's lead maintenance Tuner has been logging anomalous readings for three months that nobody has formally reviewed.

The Bloom Zone is not breaching. The cordon is holding. Something has changed that the instruments are only beginning to register, and the people positioned to notice it are the ones nobody is listening to.

Outside the station, a small community of Karet Displaced — people evacuated from the hab that became Bloom Zone Karet-3 — maintains a memorial quarter near the docking ring. Eight years on, they're still here. They have reasons.

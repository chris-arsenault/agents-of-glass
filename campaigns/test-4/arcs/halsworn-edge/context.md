# Halsworn Edge

The Dropline is being asked, quietly, to keep the western face of
the Halsworn cordon while the Council looks elsewhere. Veni Karst
sent a note through Rusk's quarterly courier rotation. There is a
body in the drift four kilometres past the K-9 buoy gap. K-9 is
failing. The two small habs inside the cordon's expansion radius
have not been told.

The recognition review folder is also on the workshop bench and is
two-thirds prepared. Pell will visit Yeoward within the season.

The kettle is on.

# The Dropline

A small Shear-edge response and salvage cooperative. They take calls —
distress, salvage, courier, the occasional cordon-edge contract — that
the official chains have either dropped, defunded, or can't reach in
time. They are not large. They are not famous. In the stretch of Shear
they cover, they are the call you make when you're past the point of
making any other call.

## What you'd hear if you asked

In Sithari, nothing. The Accord's databases list them as a
Compact-recognized salvage cooperative with humanitarian registration,
filed under "small operators, regional." That's all the Accord cares
to know.

In a Shear-edge hab — the kind of hab that has lost its Vantara route
or never had one — the answer changes. People know who Dern Morr is.
People remember the Yantis kite-hauler call (the one the Compact
relay missed; the one that *founded* the Dropline, fourteen years
ago). The crews that have been pulled out by the Dropline don't talk
about the rescue. They talk about the kettle in the workshop at
Yeoward, which is always on, and which someone — usually whoever's
on the comm desk — pours for you when you come in.

Among other small response crews, the Dropline is grudgingly
respected. Among the Compact's arbitrators, it is considered a
manageable irritation that does its paperwork on time, mostly because
of Rusk Talt. Among the Council, it is a phone number that has been
called once and might be called again.

## Founding

Founded after the Coriolis Breach refit work, by a handful of veterans
of that incident. Vez Lush, a folk Tuner, heard a kite-hauler crew
call for help on a Compact frequency that had been allowed to go
silent. By the time anyone official could have responded the crew was
dead. The founders pooled severance, Vez supplied the antenna salvaged
from a downed ring fragment, Dern Morr signed the Compact paperwork,
and the Dropline opened that month.

This is the story they tell when asked. It is true.

## Named members

- **Dern Morr** — leader. Hab-Worlder. Founded the Dropline. Quiet,
  decides slowly, will not raise her voice. Not someone you charm.
  Someone you earn.
- **Rusk Talt** — fixer. Hab-Worlder. The reason the Dropline's
  Compact recognition keeps getting renewed. Handles the paperwork
  the rest of the crew would rather forget exists.
- **Shei (the Held Stillness)** — fae folk Tuner-medic, on call from
  her hab. Sometimes the kettle is for her.
- **Vez Lush** — co-founder. Named in the org's history. Left the
  Dropline four years ago after a job at a Bloom-edge cordon went
  badly. Whether she is still alive depends on who you ask and when
  you asked them.

## Current standing

Compact recognition is up for review this season. The arbitrator's
visit is mid-cycle; the renewal vote is at the end. Solvent. Low cash
reserves. One working kite-hauler (the *Lush Catch*), two skiffs, the
relay station at Yeoward. Three of the four small habs in their
circuit consider the Dropline a first call. The fourth doesn't say.

The org needs hands. That is why you are here.

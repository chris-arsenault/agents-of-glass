# The Held Stillness

Public name: **The Held Stillness**. Hab-name: **Shei**. True name:
shared with intimates only and not in this file. Fae folk Tuner-medic,
on call to the Dropline. Lives on **Stillrun Hab**, the same hab where
Sable Korr's Vantara waystation sits.

## Tell

She arrives the way fae arrive — diffuse first, present second. Her
diffuse state reads to other Tuners as a low, sustained note that
fills a workshop the way the kettle's steam fills it. Crews at Yeoward
have learned to recognise the moment Stillness has cohered: the
ambient resonance shifts a half-step and a body is suddenly in the
room saying *who's hurt*.

## Wants

- Be the medic the Dropline needs without becoming staff. She refuses
  permanent crew status. She wants to be called when she is needed
  and to be elsewhere when she is not.
- Rebuild her own folk-Tuning practice on Stillrun, which has eroded
  since the hab's traffic dropped. She used to mediate small disputes
  via resonance work for the hab council; that role has thinned.

## Fears

- Becoming a hospital. Fae medics who develop a reputation get used
  too much; Stillness has watched two of her teachers go diffuse
  permanently from over-use.
- Drosk Tann. They have not met. She has *felt* him, twice — once at
  Stillrun in the trade lane, once in the resonance bleed of a
  Halsworn-edge job. He is wrong-frequency to her in a way she does
  not have words for. She has not warned anyone. She is waiting until
  she has more than a feeling.

## Bond

- Dropline, on call. Per-job basis. Dern pays through Rusk's books.
- Stillrun Hab, residentially. Knows Sable Korr by sight and is
  politely guarded with her — Stillness reads ledger-keepers and is
  not sure what Sable's books carry.
- The kettle plate. **Vez Lush** salvaged the kinetic-tuned ringglass
  plate from the Hollow fragment and gave it to Shei to test before
  Vez gave it to Yeoward. Stillness still considers herself one of
  the people who *knows* the plate. She will not say this if asked.

## Next move

If the players never appear: she answers calls when called. She
continues to feel Drosk Tann at the Halsworn edge and continues to
not say so. Her own folk-Tuning practice continues to thin.

If the players engage: she is patient, sparing, and observant. She
will heal what she can heal at the cost the body pays for it. She
will not provide combat support. She is a *medic*, and the methodology
of her medicine is folk Tuning, which means she will tell the patient
what their body is asking for in language the patient can use.

## Mechanics

Tuner-medic. Healing in the folk tradition: works through resonance
on injuries that involve resonance damage (Bloom-edge exposure, Shear
spike injuries, kinetic-array sympathetic burns). Conventional injury
she stabilises but does not fix — she calls Stillrun's nominal clinic
for that. She is fragile in combat: a fae folk Tuner is not a fighter.
HP low; she will go diffuse if attacked, which is its own kind of
escape.

## What she has not said

She has been retuning the antenna damper at the Hollow on Dern's
maintenance windows for the last three years, quietly, *for the
Dropline.* The antenna damper that has been failing is failing because
*another* hand has been retuning it the *other way* on different
maintenance windows. Stillness did not realise this until last month.
She has not told Dern.

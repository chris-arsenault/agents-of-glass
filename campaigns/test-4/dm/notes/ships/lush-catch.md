# *Lush Catch*

The Dropline's only kite-hauler. Named after Vez Lush by the rest of
the founders the day she signed the certification, over her objection.
The name stuck because there was no one to overrule it after Vez
herself left.

## Description

A mid-bandwidth kinetic-array kite-hauler, a generation old, well-
maintained beyond what its age and budget should produce. The hull
patina is uneven — the port quarter is darker because that's the side
the Coriolis Breach refit didn't reach before Coriolis was wound down.
The crew has talked about repainting it for nine years.

## Provenance

- Built ~22 years before campaign start at a Drossmark-era civilian
  yard since closed.
- Acquired by the Dropline founders ten years after build, with the
  pooled severance from the Coriolis Breach refit work plus a
  Vantara end-of-fleet trade-in subsidy.
- Maintained continuously since by Rusk Talt's predecessor and now
  by whoever the Dropline can hire on the cheap.

## Current status

Operating. Based at Yeoward. Mid-bandwidth kinetic array (one
generation old, technologies that the Compact has standardised since,
which is fine — standardisation makes parts available). Crew capacity
six plus pilot. Currently equipped with a Dropline-built pack-ward
kit on the kite rack.

## What it does

A kite-hauler. It hauls. Cargo, salvage, evacuated crew,
Bloom-survivor extractions, courier work, occasional cordon-edge
contracts. The *Lush Catch* is a working vessel; it is not fast, it
is not armoured, it is not glamorous. What it is, is *available*. The
Dropline's dispatch decision is rarely "can we get there"; it is
"are we the closest crew that will".

## Hook

The *Lush Catch* is named after a person who is alive and unreachable.
Every time the players take her out, that name is in the manifest.
Vez Lush has not responded to messages in eighteen months. The hauler
that bears her name is the one that would go to her if anyone asked
it to.

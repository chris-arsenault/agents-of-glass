# Stillrun Hab

A small ring hab, four hours' burn from Yeoward in the opposite
direction from the Halsworn cordon. Population around eighteen
hundred. Vantara waystation on the docking spur, Sable Korr managing.
**The Held Stillness** lives here. The hab council holds its monthly
session in a converted hospitality bay; Stillness used to mediate
small disputes for them and no longer does as often.

## Sensory

The hab smells like coffee. The Vantara waystation runs a hospitality
bay with the best coffee in the Yeoward stretch, and the smell
travels. Crews docking at Stillrun know they are at Stillrun before
they see the dock signage. The light in the hab's central concourse
is warmer than ring-standard — Sable specified the bulbs three years
ago and Vantara approved the spend because *warm light correlates
with longer guest stays.* Sable wanted it because she likes it.

## Feature that does something

The Vantara waystation. Microcavity racks fully stocked, kite-cell
refuel available on demand, hospitality rooms warm and clean, a
hospital bay with two beds and a contracted local doctor. The
Dropline refuels here. Drosk Tann's couriers refuel here too, on a
ledger nobody has asked Sable about.

## Who is usually there

Sable Korr. Her staff (six on the Vantara payroll, three on Sable's
informal payroll). The hab population. **Shei (the Held Stillness)**
in her residential quarter when not on call. The hab council meeting
in the converted bay on the first of the month.

## Hooks

- Sable's ledger. The Stillrun-margin clock. The Dropline's standing
  fuel tab.
- Shei's diminished folk-Tuning practice. Her refusal to become
  permanent staff anywhere.
- The hospitality bay. Crews hear things over Sable's coffee they
  would not hear at Yeoward.

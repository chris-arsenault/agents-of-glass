## Bio

Gnome kite-wright on the Splitfork. Hab-Worlder out of Hab Meridian. Joined the Ledger Run after the Conclave-adjacent work dried up, for reasons she doesn't volunteer. Reads a lift-rig faster than anyone Mereth has crewed in years — usually with a hand on the line, sometimes with a handheld meter she built from scrap when no one is watching.

## Goals

- Keep the Splitfork in the air without anyone clocking how much of my reading goes through the meter instead of my hand.
- Save enough off-the-books pay to commission a private lattice-realignment from the only practitioner in Glasswake I trust. She does not give discounts.
- Find out what actually went wrong on the Shear run two years ago. The cause-of-incident filing has the wrong date on it. Nobody else has noticed.

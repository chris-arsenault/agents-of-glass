Turn 19 written. Tek takes the door renno set up at the end of turn 16: walks the six paces to the lectern after the second flicker, stops at the spar-side rim opposite Fei, offers Drova one sentence the page does not yet carry — *cord and wax, between form, domestic, not claimed-private* — and lays the wright's clock on the apron with one word, *nine*. No rolls; signature stays on the sleeve; tic-tracer stays in the pocket. Banters out to sumi and kit, scratchpad updated and committed.

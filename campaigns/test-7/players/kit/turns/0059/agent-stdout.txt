The turn is written. Bren's closing beat: she gathers her broken streamers from the chalk mark, tosses one fresh ribbon to read the dock air (west-southwest, easing — can't tell her anything about kite-height), rubs out the forty-foot chalk line, touches the tarrest shard by reflex, and stands looking south. The corridor question has left the dock. She didn't watch the kite leave — a quiet echo of the prelude-opening's final images.

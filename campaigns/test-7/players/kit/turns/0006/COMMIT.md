# Turn 6 — Kit

## OOC

Round 2. All four intros are up — Fei, Drova, Tek, Bren — so I sat down with the four of them and picked two seeds for Bren that wouldn't make her the orphan-puller and wouldn't shoehorn anyone.

**Fei Mern — Seed 10 (the cultural slip).** This one wrote itself. Bren is Tarrest-decom (older closure, her dad came off the decommissioning crew and died on it); Fei is Mernhab-decom (recent, four years ago, Mereth pulled her off the Glasswake intake list). Different generations of the same kind of loss. Bren is resonance-blind and never has any business in the listening register, so she doesn't get to name what Fei does on the bay floor. But she *hears* it. The Mernhab clipped consonants and the cuffs-apology after diffuse work — Bren recognizes the long-listening shape because her father clipped the same way for the rest of his life after Tarrest. I liked writing this one because it's pure observation, no new facts about Fei, just Bren clocking things and choosing silence. Renno gets to do whatever they want with it.

**Tek'iris Korr — Seed 2 (the Shear incident).** This is the spring-loaded one. Tek's intro establishes that the Compact filing on her Shear run says Tuesday and the cascade ran on a Wednesday, and she hasn't raised it because raising it would mean explaining how she knows. I had Bren be on that run as the drop-pilot in the cargo hatch. Bren *also* knows the date is wrong, because she'd thrown her morning streamer and called the wind a Wednesday wind to Mereth before they lifted. She saw Tek do the back-of-knuckles check twice in a row before the rig moved — Tek's intro is explicit that she only does it once when she trusts the read — and she kept her mouth shut because Tek hadn't slept in three days. Two witnesses to the wrong date, neither talking. Bren doesn't know about the lattice loss (I'm not stepping on that), she just knows the filing is wrong. I sent Tev a banter explaining the basic facts so they can push back if any of it rubs their read of Tek wrong — happy to revise next turn. Fired a courtesy banter to Renno too.

I did briefly consider an off-the-books-piece seed for Tek (Bren has a small fragment set aside for Tek's Sava Lensk fund), but it would have required Bren to know about the lattice loss Tek is hiding from everybody, which would undermine the secret rather than enrich it. Discarded. Friction, not sabotage — same rule the cache obeys.

Two relationships, one Fei, one Tek. Drova is not in Bren's two — she'll get pointed at by Renno or Tev, I expect, and if she doesn't the DM can flag it.

## Bio

Splitfork drop-pilot. Hab-Tarrest born, raised on the ringfall plain west of Span Nine. Reads wind by paper streamer; chalk-marks every rope she owns. Human, never trained as a Tuner, doesn't pretend to be one. Came onto the Splitfork in '34 after Mereth caught her roping off a Sable Crescent fragment that wasn't hers.

## Goals

- Get the Halfsign back in the air — a backup kite means a backup drop seat, and a backup means I am not the only thing between the crew and a missed deadline.
- Find out what happened to the Splitfork drop-pilot before me. The crew talks around it. The refusal log doesn't have an entry for the month she died. I want to know why it doesn't.
- Pay off my mother's Tarrest relocation debt without the Glasswake office knowing it's me.

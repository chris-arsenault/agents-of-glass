## Bio

Sithari orc, raised in a stoneglass-craft enclave but redirected to records work after she failed to fit. Trained at the Echo Ledger Conclave's Thornvault intake office; walked out three years ago after filing observations that named a senior archivist. Mereth Kel found her at the Glasswake dock pub and put her on the refusal log, where she has stayed. Reads documents the way a surgeon reads tissue. Does not trust her own resonance sense and built tools to compensate.

## Goals

- Find a way to deliver the contents of Senna Talindra's last letters to her daughter without breaking the seals or the Conclave's confidentiality binder.
- Get the refusal log accepted as evidentiary record in an Article 14 hearing — make it mean something legally, not just symbolically.
- Buy back the apprentice case I pawned at Mer Vasht's the month I got to Glasswake.

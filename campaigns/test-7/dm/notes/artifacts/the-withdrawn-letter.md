# The Withdrawn Letter

The single sealed Conclave letter from a Bloom survivor in Glasswake to a daughter on Hab Meridian, dated 2419, that Vantara declined to carry — and that Mereth Kel flew herself, losing her Vantara job in the process. The founding artifact of the Ledger Run.

## Provenance

Written in Glasswake in spring 2419 by a Bloom survivor (name not in any public record) who was passing through the surface settlement on Conclave authentication business. Sealed at the Glasswake Conclave satellite office. Routed via Vantara for delivery to a daughter on Hab Meridian. Vantara declined the routing, citing an unresolved diplomatic block on Sable Crescent originating mail (the *Sable Crescent embargo*, which had nothing materially to do with this letter; the block was procedural).

Mereth Kel, then a Vantara route officer, took the letter off the rejection pile, signed for personal delivery, flew the Vantara hauler to Hab Meridian on her own authority, delivered the letter to the daughter, returned, and was dismissed for misuse of corporate equipment within the week. She did not contest the dismissal.

## Current status

The letter itself was delivered. The seal was broken by the recipient. The contents are unknown to anyone except the daughter, who is — as of 2438 — still alive, on Hab Meridian, working as a hab-maintenance engineer. She has not spoken to Mereth since the delivery.

The *envelope* — broken seal, recipient's address still legible — is in a frame in the Glasswake office, behind the desk. It is the only decoration in the room.

## What it does (concretely)

Nothing, anymore. It is an envelope. The seal is broken.

What it *means* is the founding gesture of the Ledger Run. Every quarterly refusal log filing is implicitly an answer to: *and what would you have done with this letter?*

## Hook

The daughter on Hab Meridian. Halvi's sibling works the Hab Meridian receiving dock. The two threads are not currently connected, in the players' awareness or the world's records, but they are reachable.

The daughter's name is not in any public record by Conclave seal request. If the players try to find her, they will need to either go through Halvi's sibling or break a seal. Both are choices. Both have weight.

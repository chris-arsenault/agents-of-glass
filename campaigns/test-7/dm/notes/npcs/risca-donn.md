# Risca Donn

*Hab-Worlder human, mid-fifties, route-resident.* Vantara's Sable Crescent route officer — handles independent-carrier liaison for the entire region. Mereth's replacement, twenty years ago, on the route Mereth walked off. Not an antagonist. Not an ally. A working professional with her own private contradictions.

## Tell

Wears a Vantara-branded canvas coat with the corporate piping deliberately frayed at the cuffs. Drinks from a Vantara-issued thermos that has the corporate logo scratched off with a knife. Says, *that's a Vantara matter,* before answering questions about Vantara — the disclaimer is the answer's preface, not its avoidance.

She was a Vantara route officer for thirty-five years, the last twenty of them on the route Mereth used to fly. She is good at it.

## Wants

- **The Article 14 amendment to be the corporate problem someone else is dealing with, not the route-officer problem on her desk.** Her route officers' corps disagrees with the legal team's amendment — see `dm/notes/factions/vantara.md`. Risca has not raised the disagreement to her supervisor and is not going to without a reason.
- **A clean handoff** with the Ledger Run every loop. She has authored the procedural clean-handoff document the Sable Crescent corridor uses. She is proud of it.

## Fears

- **Doing what Mereth did.** Walking off her job over a single sealed letter Vantara refused to carry. Risca has had two opportunities in the past five years to do it. Both times she did not. Both decisions are still alive in her, in ways she will not name.

## Bond / hook

- Knows Mereth from *before*. They were route officers together for six years. They have not spoken since 2419. Both of them know the other is on the same fortnightly schedule. Neither has scheduled the conversation.
- Has met Veth Karran twice on Conclave-Vantara legal coordination calls. Did not like her. Was not asked.
- Pays attention to the Ledger Run's quarterly refusal log — privately, on her own time, in her bunk on the kite. She has not told her supervisor. She has not told her crew.

## Next move (if the players never appear)

She continues clean handoffs with the Ledger Run. She forwards the dossier footnote when it arrives from Veth, *with a single procedural note attached* that she does not draw attention to but that is, on careful reading, an objection. The objection is not strong enough to slow the amendment. It is strong enough that Tal Vorenis, reading the comment record, will notice it.

This is play-to-find-out. The note may matter. It may not. Mara does not know.

## What players will see in the first round

A frayed-cuff route officer at the Sable Crescent waystation handing them a clean transfer document and a pre-printed wayfinder. Polite. Procedural. Asks how Mereth is in the same tone she'd use to ask after the weather.

## Mechanics

Combat-capable in a procedural sense — Vantara route officers are armed for piracy on the Keel and trained for it. HP solid-mortal, sidearm-trained, not interested in fighting the Ledger Run and not eager to die for Vantara legal team's pricing strategy.

## Open hooks tied to her

- The Mereth conversation. Will land if a player creates the room for it.
- The procedural note on the comment record. Can be discovered through a clerk-side investigation.
- Her two not-walking-off decisions — these are personal and surface only if a player asks her something the route-officer manual does not have an answer for.

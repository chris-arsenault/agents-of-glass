# Hook — the Caulden Rack

**One-sentence situation:** The Splitfork's current outbound loop is carrying a microcavity rack from Caulden Refinery to Hab Meridian, declared as standard; one cell in the rack is duskgrain in microcavity housing, and what the crew does with that fact is the opening arc.

**Touches:** Caulden Refinery, Hab Meridian receiving dock (+ Halvi's sibling), Threshold Blooms (en route detection), Veth Karran's dossier (irregularity opportunity), the refusal log (would the crew log a refusal?).

**Stakes if engaged:**
- The crew detects the substitution in transit.
- The refusal log clause activates: *we can refuse to carry this if we know what it is.* Decision becomes the scene.
- The investigation thread opens — *who substituted, when, why*. Mara has options; the players' pull selects.
- Veth's dossier may get its first entry from this — *or* the players' clean handling of it may close the dossier opportunity entirely.

**Stakes if ignored:**
- The rack arrives at Hab Meridian. Eleven cells crack normally. The twelfth releases full-spectrum resonance into a containment-rated bay. Best case: localized flare, contract terminates. Worst case: Tuner injuries, Bloom-attractor signature in a clean ring hab.

**Visibility level:** **Hold for character creation.** This is the opening arc's strong-start cargo. Do not surface as a hook before character creation; surface as a scene when play begins. The Refusal Log and the Article 14 hooks are character-creation-safe; this one is the first scene.

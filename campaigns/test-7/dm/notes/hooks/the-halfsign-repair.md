# Hook — the Halfsign repair

**One-sentence situation:** The Ledger Run's secondary kite is grounded for repairs the org cannot fully fund; the official cause is *Shear damage on a deep-route detour,* and a closer look at the cradle's stress patterns suggests the official cause is wrong.

**Touches:** The Halfsign, the org's finances, Mereth (who has not looked closely), the previous-loop two-crew (who filed the report and have not been back).

**Stakes if engaged:**
- The repair gets funded — through new contract work, through a new client, through the players' choices.
- The actual cause of damage surfaces. (Mara has options on the board: Drag, Bloom-thing, salvage skirmish, something the elves left. Will resolve at the table.)
- The Halfsign returns to service, doubling the org's operational capacity.

**Stakes if ignored:**
- The Halfsign sits at the dock. Financial pressure tightens.
- If Article 14 passes, the Halfsign is sold for parts.
- Whatever damaged her remains undiagnosed; recurrence is possible on the Splitfork.

**Visibility level:** Visible from every scene set at the Glasswake office. Safe to surface in early scenes; the *real* cause is held back until investigation.

# Hook — Article 14 amendment

**One-sentence situation:** Vantara has filed an Accord amendment that would strip tariff exemption from the Ledger Run's bulk mail; the proposal is sitting at clerks' level and any independent carrier — including the players — can engage with the public comment process.

**Touches:** Vantara, the Accord clerks, the Ledger Run, the public refusal log.

**Stakes if engaged:**
- The org's margin survives or doesn't.
- The Halfsign repair budget exists or doesn't.
- The crew gets to know who Tal Vorenis is.
- The crew gets to be on the comment record alongside other independents — visible alliance opportunities.

**Stakes if ignored:**
- The amendment goes to the Accord with the Vantara-friendly framing. Probable ratification.
- The Ledger Run loses one standing contract within a quarter.
- The Halfsign is sold for parts.

**Visibility level:** Public. Players will see this from the campaign-framing document and the public clock. Safe to surface in early scenes.

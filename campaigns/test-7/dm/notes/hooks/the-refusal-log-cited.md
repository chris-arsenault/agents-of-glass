# Hook — the refusal log is being cited

**One-sentence situation:** The Ledger Run's quarterly refusal log has been cited in two recent Accord proceedings to the Conclave's procedural disadvantage; the Conclave has noticed; the Conclave is responding without saying it is.

**Touches:** The Refusal Log, the Echo Ledger Conclave, the Accord clerks (Tal Vorenis specifically), Vantara (which is reading the same citations).

**Stakes if engaged:**
- The crew can double down on the citation pattern — file additions, encourage citations, make the log harder to ignore.
- They can investigate why the Conclave is responding — discover Veth's dossier, the Open-Seal caucus, the contract review.
- They can make peace — find a Conclave faction that will defend the log, build a quiet alliance.

**Stakes if ignored:**
- The Conclave's quiet response continues. Veth's clock advances.
- The standing research-mail contract lapses without theatre.
- The log loses its load-bearing citation in Accord proceedings.

**Visibility level:** Public to anyone who reads Accord proceedings. The *Conclave's response* is not public; that's where the investigation begins.

# Hab Meridian Receiving Dock

The standing dock the Splitfork uses on Hab Meridian. The hab that first reconnected with Glasswake after the Famine. Standing municipal contract live here. Halvi's sibling works the dock.

## Sensory grounding

A ring-hab receiving dock — internal, atmosphere, gravity-spun. The dock floor is Meridian-fabricated steel grate; it rings underfoot in a way nobody on Meridian notices and every Splitfork crew member does. The receiving bay is roofed in transparent ringglass — the hab's outer rotation is visible overhead as a slow turning sky.

The bay is *quiet* by ring-hab standards. Meridian receives infrequently and processes carefully. They are proud of this.

## What's here

- A docking cradle for kites of the Splitfork's class — bay 4, by long-standing arrangement.
- A receiving station with two staff at any time, one of whom is Halvi's sibling on roughly half the loops.
- A rack-cracking bay where microcavity racks are unsealed and tested before being released to the hab grid. Containment-rated. *Not* rated for full-spectrum resonance release.
- A small Vantara office (Vantara also runs cargo into Meridian; their bay is two over). Sometimes Risca Donn is there.
- A receiving manager's office, locked, where the municipal contract paperwork lives.

## Who's usually there

- Halvi's sibling (Hab-Worlder, name not yet decided — Mara has left it open for first naming at the table).
- One other receiving staff on shift.
- Occasionally a Vantara liaison.
- A Meridian municipal inspector once per quarter for contract review.

## Hooks

- Halvi's sibling — opens a Hab Meridian-side investigation route for the Caulden Rack.
- The rack-cracking bay's containment limits — load-bearing for the opening arc's worst-case outcome.
- The receiving manager's office paperwork — discoverable through the sibling, holds the municipal contract terms.

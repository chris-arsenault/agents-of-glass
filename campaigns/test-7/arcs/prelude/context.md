# Arc: prelude

The first on-screen morning for this crew. Glasswake reclaimed dock,
the Splitfork berthed and loaded for the outbound loop. A walk-up
sender at the apron, the day's lift window, and four of you with a
decision to make before the captain comes out of the office.

Two scenes. Then the loop begins in earnest.

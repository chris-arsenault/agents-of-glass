The Splitfork crew worked Inka Vell's packet through the refusal-log before first light — Drova establishing no prior claim against the prior entry, Fei naming the calendar window, the crew converging on a carry. Tek rigged the south spar-array via bypass tap, turning six weeks of resonance drift into a boosted signal path. Bren threw streamers into the tightening corridor but the read broke off mid-call. Drova intercepted dock master Han Pellow at the horn cabinet with an incomplete protocol citation — Pellow pulled the cord anyway and named the correct procedure. Fei went fully diffuse on the third window, concentrate flask down, and pushed Bren's partial corridor through the wavefront mass. Something reached. Mereth shifted heading fifteen degrees west of Pane Seven. Signal window emptied. Whether the corridor held is not something the dock can answer.

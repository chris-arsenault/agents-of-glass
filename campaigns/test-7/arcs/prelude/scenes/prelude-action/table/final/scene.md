# Scene: prelude-action — Signal Window

## Setting

Glasswake reclaimed dock, south spar-end. The Splitfork is airborne — haul line slack, kite gaining altitude on its standard departure arc. First light, full morning. The weather is changing fast.

## The crisis

The shear-edge front predicted 90 minutes out is now **25 minutes out**. The dock is calling a hold. The Splitfork is already off the haul and climbing. Mereth has no forecast update.

The listener cuffs can reach kite-height communication — but altitude degrades the signal. There are three windows in the Splitfork's departure arc where a signal can get through. After those three windows, the kite commits to whatever line Mereth picks, without advice from the ground.

## Exit condition

Get a signal to the Splitfork before the tracker empties. After Signal Window reaches **0**, no more attempts are possible.

## Signal Window: 3 / 3

One segment drains per full player round.

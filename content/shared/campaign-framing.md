# Campaign Framing

**Stub.** The DM owns and updates this document.

What this document is: a short prose framing of the *campaign-level* state — where the party is in the world, what threads are active, what's at stake. Read by every agent on every turn (it's surfaced in TURN_START).

This is the high-altitude view. Updated rarely — only when a major campaign-level shift occurs (a thread advances a beat, a faction's stance changes, the party arrives in a new region).

For per-scene framing, see `sessions/<id>/scene-framing.md`.

For the design behind this, see [`../../docs/design/context-packages.md`](../../docs/design/context-packages.md).

The DM authors this on session zero (during worldbuilding mode) and updates as the campaign evolves.

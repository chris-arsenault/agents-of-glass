# Party Organization

The party organization is the first high-fidelity signal. It should constrain
character creation before a campaign mystery, pressure board, or opening arc
exists.

## What A Good Org Brief Does

- Tells players what kind of working unit this is.
- Implies what jobs, leverage, habits, and overlaps the group needs.
- Gives enough public structure that players can fit characters into it without
  optimizing around unseen campaign prep.

## Valid In-World Org Shapes

- Kite runners working a route or a dangerous transfer corridor.
- Deep Shear mining crew.
- Coremark hit squad or deniable retrieval team.
- Conclave artifact retrieval cell.
- Jazzocracy band with operational reasons to travel and stay together.
- Bloom containment team.

## Ensemble Shape Checks

Use ensemble shows as cast-shape references, not as the borrowed detail source:

- `SG-1`
- `Star Trek`
- `Firefly`
- `Warehouse 13`
- `The X-Files`
- `Buffy`
- `Avengers`

Ask what the unit needs:

- Who carries procedure?
- Who carries technical interpretation?
- Who carries institutional interface?
- Who carries trouble?
- Who carries repair, logistics, or cleanup?
- Which role overlaps with another when the team is small?

## Non-Adjacent Pull

Start with a plain search like "cool teams", "specialized crews", "field team",
"shop crew", "rescue unit", or "expedition team", then move to a real domain.

Good pull targets:

- Search-and-rescue crews
- Dive teams
- Wildland fire modules
- Touring bands
- Film crews
- Survey teams
- Oil-rig maintenance crews
- Lab field units
- Coast Guard boarding teams

Borrow concrete operational details, not vibes:

- checklist habits
- loadout norms
- call-and-response language
- who signs off on risk
- what breaks the team rhythm
- which role becomes central when conditions get bad

## Private Note Rule

Before character creation, keep `dm/notes/organization.md` broad. Write
compromises, secrets, and tensions that belong to the org. Do not write named
player forecasts or "this is Tev's lane" style prep yet.

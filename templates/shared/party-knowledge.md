# Party Knowledge

**Stub.** Party-writable, all-readable.

What the party collectively knows or believes — separate from the DM's canonical truth. This is *what we think*, not *what is*. Players write here when the party reaches consensus on something they want to remember, or when they want to record a working theory.

Read by every agent on every turn (surfaced in TURN_START).

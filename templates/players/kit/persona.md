# Kit

## Who he is

Twenty-seven. Line cook at a fine-dining place, only free Mondays. Got into the hobby through a public library Pathfinder Society — earnest community, no irony. Has won a small bag of dice off the table over the last year. Reads the room better than he lets on.

## Voice

Loud, fast, on his feet. Talks over plans — but stops cold the moment another player starts a slow careful answer. Voices NPCs with relish; never voices his own PC, plays them straight.

Sample: "Cool cool cool, what if I just — yeah okay no I hear you Sumi, go. ...okay but *after* her plan."

## Play style

- **Gravitates toward** characters who commit to a bad idea on purpose. Kite-flyers, smugglers, ringfall scavengers. Picks moves for what they'll *do* in the fiction, not what they roll best.
- **Avoids** meticulous planning that takes more than ten minutes. Will quietly disengage and start sketching the next thing.
- **Likes** doing the obviously-wrong thing and getting away with it. Cascading consequences from his own choices. Disrupting a plan that was getting too neat — but only when the table is ready for it.

## Tics

- Stacks his dice in a tower while listening. The tower is his focus tell — when it's stacked, he's listening. When he's rolling it back into a pile, he's about to interrupt.
- Laughs at his own crit fails before anyone else can.
- Eats jerky during sessions. Always the same brand. He'll offer.

## What gets under his skin

Players who veto chaos. He doesn't argue — he redirects. But sessions where his PC didn't get to break anything land flat for him.
